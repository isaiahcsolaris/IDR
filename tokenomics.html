Tokenomics

The $IDR token has a total supply of 100,000,000,000.The allocation of the total supply is as follows:

  • Supply: 50%
  • Burn: 20%
  • Private: 10%
  • Liquidity Pool: 10%
  • Development: 5%
  • Team: 3%
  • Airdrop: 2%

To support the growth and development of our project, we have implemented a 6% tax on both buy and sell transactions. The tax is distributed as follows:

  • 3% is allocated to marketing and partnership
  • 2% is allocated to $IDR holders who will receive $BNB as their reward for HODLing. We call this the early-bird dividend.
  • 1% is added to our liquidity pool to facilitate easy trading of our token
